Trinity College Library Dublin, Ireland

This library is home to one of the worlds oldest manuscript, Books of Kells which was illuminted by monks in the year 800. Trinity College Library also posesses the Ussher Collection which they acquired in 1661 and the Fagel Collection which they acquired in 1802. The library has beautiful, grand wooden arches and marble busts along the first floor.

Strahov Library Strahov, Czech Republic

It was built over many years but was eventually completed in the late 18th century. The Strahov Monastery library has well over 42,000 volumes and, like many other libraries built around that time, incorporated the use of frescoes to decorate the interior.

Library of Congress Washignton,D.C.

Building started in 1873 and was evetually finished in 1897 and was proclaimed the largest library in the world. Much like the Trinity College Library, the Library of Congress is also responsible for helpig people receive copyrights on their works. .
